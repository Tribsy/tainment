What it does

Everything your server needs

🪙

Economy

Coins, Gems, and Tokens. Work, gamble, rob, and trade your way up the server leaderboard.

🎮

Games

Trivia, Hangman, Wordle, Blackjack, Tic-Tac-Toe, Connect Four, and more — all earning real rewards.

🎣

Fishing

2300+ fish across 10 rarity tiers. 11 rod types unlock higher tiers. Premium Bait boosts rare catches for 1 hour.

⌨️

Fun Games

Typerace, riddles, Would You Rather, Emoji Decode, Hot Potato, Word Chain — new ways to earn daily.

🛡️

Moderation

Full mod suite: warn, kick, ban, timeout, purge, slowmode, lock/unlock, nick, and a full case log.

🏆

Levels & Roles

XP per message, 10 milestone roles from Newcomer (Lvl 5) to Void Walker (Lvl 300). Auto-created on server join.

🎂

Birthdays

Per-server birthday tracking. Daily announcements in your chosen channel with a birthday gift of 500 coins + 5 gems.

⚙️

Server Settings

Custom prefix per server, AFK system, command toggles per channel, addemote, randomcolor, and server subscription tiers.

🎁

Giveaways & Polls

Timed giveaways with multiple winners, community polls, and personal reminders.

🛒

Premium Shop

60+ items — rods, profile cosmetics, XP boosts, streak shields, mystery boxes, piggy banks, music boosts, and more.

🎵

Music System

Discover songs by genre & mood, share tracks, compete in lyrics guessing, music trivia, and build personal playlists — all with coin rewards.

🤖

AutoMod

Per-guild automated moderation: word filter, anti-spam, link control, caps limit, mention flood protection. Actions: warn, timeout, or kick.
