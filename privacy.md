# Privacy Policy — Tainment+

**Effective date: 22 March 2026**

---

## 1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains what data Tainment+ collects, how it is used, and your rights around that data. We are committed to collecting only what is necessary to power the bot's features.

## 2. Data we collect

When you interact with Tainment+, the following data may be stored in our database:

- Your Discord user ID and username (for account identification)
- Economy data — coin, gem, and token balances, transaction history
- Fishing stats — catches, rods owned, fishing XP and level
- Level and XP data per server
- Subscription tier and payment reference IDs (no card details — payments are handled by LemonSqueezy)
- Music profile data — favourite genre, shared tracks, playlist names
- Inventory items purchased from the shop
- Server settings — prefix, command toggles, configured channels, server subscription tier
- Birthday date (only if you set it via `t!birthday set`)
- Reminders you create via `t!remind`
- AFK status messages

## 3. Data we do not collect

- Message content beyond the bot command itself
- Real names, email addresses, or any personal identifying information
- Payment card details (handled entirely by LemonSqueezy)
- Data from servers the bot is not in
- Voice data of any kind

## 4. How we use your data

Data is used exclusively to power Tainment+'s features:

- Tracking economy balances and transactions
- Displaying profiles, leaderboards, and stats
- Delivering reminders and birthday announcements
- Applying the correct subscription tier and its benefits
- Enforcing per-server settings and command toggles

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties, except as described in section 5.

## 5. Third-party services

**LemonSqueezy** — subscription payments are processed by LemonSqueezy. When you initiate a purchase, a checkout is created containing your Discord user ID and chosen tier. LemonSqueezy's own Privacy Policy (https://www.lemonsqueezy.com/privacy) applies to that transaction.

**Discord** — the bot operates within Discord's platform. Discord's own Privacy Policy (https://discord.com/privacy) governs how Discord handles your data.

## 6. Data retention

Your data is retained for as long as you or your server actively uses Tainment+. If you would like your data deleted, contact us via the support server and we will remove your user record within 14 days. Note that deleting your data will permanently reset your economy, level, subscriptions, and all other bot progress.

## 7. Security

Data is stored in a local SQLite database on a private server. We take reasonable precautions to protect your data, but no system is completely secure. Use the bot at your own risk.

## 8. Children's privacy

Tainment+ is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from users under 13. If you believe a child has provided data, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

## 9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect the latest revision. Continued use of Tainment+ after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## 10. Contact

Questions or data deletion requests? Join our support server (https://discord.gg/SgkYDqkZmt) or use `t!support` in Discord.
